about 7moods:

7moods is a lifestyle brand dedicated to creative wellness and lifestyle design. Our goal is to help every people discover a moment of wellness. We work in collaboration with wellness and lifestyle brands to deliver unique and authentic experiences to our customers.

Our signature item, Chakra Care Boxes are a creative way to remind busy people about the importance of taking a moment
for self-care. Each boxes are carefully themed and curated around to help inspire focused intention on various areas in life & love.
