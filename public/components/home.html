What is a chakra?
The word chakra is Sanskrit for wheel or disk and signifies one of seven basic energy centers in the body. Chakras are said to connect and correspond with the physical, mental, emotional and spiritual aspects of our being. Each chakra is associated with with specific colors, sounds, & flowers.
